# Rules & Regulations

## Tiflan i.e Age 6 - 9

- Date of Fee Submission: 1st January 2026
- Time of Fee Submission: 08:00 PM to 10:00 PM
- Fees: PKR 300
- Parent or Guardian of student must come on day of fee submission. Anyone else from family will not be allowed to submit fee.
- No admission will be accepted after 10:00 PM on 1st January 2026.
- Admission will be given on first come first serve basis.
- Once seats are filled, no more admission will be accepted.

## Muhibaan i.e Age 10 - 17

### For Boys

- Day of Fee Submission: Saturday
- Date of Fee Submission: 1st January 2026
- Time of Fee Submission: 07:00 PM to 10:00 PM

### For Girls

- Day of Fee Submission: Sunday
- Date of Fee Submission: 1st January 2026
- Time of Fee Submission: 07:00 PM to 10:00 PM

### Common Rules

- Fees: PKR 300
- Student must come on day of fee submission. Anyone else will not be allowed to submit fee and receive card.
- No card will be issued after 10:00 PM on the day of fee submission.
- Card will be issued on first come first serve basis.
- Once seats are filled for any given class, no more cards will be issued for that class.
